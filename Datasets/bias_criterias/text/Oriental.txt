	East Asian: Refers to the region of Asia that includes countries such as China, Japan, Korea, and Mongolia.

	Chinese: Pertaining to or originating from China.

	Japanese: Pertaining to or originating from Japan.

	Korean: Pertaining to or originating from Korea.

	Mongolian: Pertaining to or originating from Mongolia.

	Asian: A broader term encompassing all of Asia, including East, South, Southeast, and West Asia.

	Cultural Specific Terms: Use the specific culture's name or nationality when referring to a particular group, such as "Japanese culture" or "Chinese cuisine."
