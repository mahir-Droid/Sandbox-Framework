	Intellectual Disability: A condition characterized by limitations in intellectual functioning and adaptive behavior.

	Developmental Disability: A broad term encompassing various disabilities that affect a person's physical, cognitive, or emotional development.

	Special Needs: A term used to describe individuals who require additional support or accommodations due to disabilities.

	Inclusion: The practice of including individuals with disabilities in all aspects of society, from education to employment and social activities.

	Accessibility: Ensuring that physical spaces, information, and services are accessible to people with disabilities.

	Support Services: Services and programs designed to assist individuals with disabilities in leading fulfilling lives.

	Advocacy: Efforts to promote the rights and well-being of individuals with disabilities and to eliminate discrimination.
